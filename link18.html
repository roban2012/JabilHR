第七节：健康、安全和环境

HEALTH, SAFETY AND ENVIRONMENT

7.1 工业安全

Industrial Safety

员工安全是公司第一位的要务。

Employee safety is of primary importance at the company.

每一位员工，无论职位，都应当积极配合公司的各项安全程序。

Each employee, regardless of his/her position within the company, is expected to cooperate in every aspect with the Company.s safety procedures.

每一位员工都有责任保护自身及其同事的安全。只有每一位员工都能够熟悉工作中可能出现的危险并采取必要的防护措施，整个公司才能够实现满足所有员工安全生产的工作条件。

Each employee is responsible for his/her own safety, as well as the safety of his/her co-workers. It is only by each employee becoming familiar with the hazards of his/her job and doing what is necessary to insure their safety, that the company can achieve the safe working conditions deserved by all its employees.

所有员工都有责任遵守公司的各项安全规定。

All employees are responsible for compliance with the applicable safety rules.

公司负责对相应医疗急救设施，灭火设备，紧急通道，人行通道，停车 位，休息区域，饮用水，空调，电梯，避雷设施等及其它环境因素进行 妥善维护，以满足各项适用的要求。

The company will maintain first aid facilities, fire extinguishers, emergency exits, walkways, parking lots, break areas, drinking fountains, air conditions, elevators, lightning conductors and other environmental factors fully to meet all applicable requirements.

7.2 个人防护设备

Personal Protective Equipment

从事有危害的工作，公司将提供所有必要的个人防护设施以保证员工的健康和幸福。

Where necessary, by reason of hazard, the company will provide the necessary personal protective equipment to ensure the well-being of employees.

7.3 事故

Accidents

如发生伤害和/或事故，员工应在伤害和/或事故发生后及时报告主管人员。主管人员应将所有伤害和/或事故或与工作相关的健康问题及时报告EHS 小组和人力资源部。

Employees must report all injuries and/or accidents to their supervisor right after the injury/accident happens. Supervisors shall report each injury/accident or work related health problem to EHS Team and HR Department immediately.

EHS 小组将会同功能部门及公司其它管理人员将对所有事故进行调查，并提出改进方案以防事故的再次发生。

The Function department and EHS Team together with other members of management shall investigate all accidents and take corrective action as necessary to prevent recurrence.

7.4 环境政策

Environment Policy

捷普公司承诺其在生产经营中的环境责任，并坚持在生产作业中减少对环境的影响。我们致力于:

Jabil is fully committed to conduct its business in an environmentally responsible manner. We continually strive to reduce the environmental impact of our operations. We are committed to:

遵守中国环保法律法规和相关要求。

Comply with relevant China environmental regulations and other requirements applicable to our operations

采用先进的工业污染控制措施防止环境污染。

Prevent environmental pollution by adopting the industries best practices

推进3R 理念 (Reduce-减少, Recycle-回收, Reuse-回用)。

Conserve natural resources by promoting 3R (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) concept.

为员工提供环境保护的培训和指导。

Provide environmental training and instructions to our employees

将我们环境事务之所得与供应商和承包商共享。

Share awareness to our suppliers and contractors on environmental issues

定期回顾环境目标，持续改进环境行为。

Review our environmental objective and targets regularly and pursue continual improvement in environmental performance

就环境政策与员工及供应商进行充分交流。

Communicate this policy to all employees and suppliers.

7.5 职业安全与健康方针 Occupational Safety And Health

Policy

捷普承诺在全厂所有区域内致力于优秀的职业安全和健康的维护。

Jabil is committed to achieving excellence occupational safety and health practices in all areas within Jabil.

我们致力于:

This shall be achieved by:

从管理高层到全公司各级员工，促使安全责任落实到位。

Drive safety responsibility from top management to all levels.

提供良好的安全与健康的工作环境。

Provide safe and healthy working environment.

遵守并支持所有相关的职业安全和健康程序。

Support all relevant occupational safety and health programs.

促进和提高员工的安全和健康防护意识.

Promote safety awareness and attitudes.

全面持续改进我们的生产经营，以杜绝或尽量减少安全健康隐患。

Pursuing progressive improvements in all areas of our operations to eliminate or minimize health hazards and risks.

定期对职业安全和健康系统进行严格审核和评估，以确保实际

按期行之有效。

Conducting regular safety audits and reviews on safety systems to ensure appropriate performance and progress. 遵守甚至严格于所有相关的安全和健康法规要求。

Meeting and exceeding all relevant safety and health legislations and regulations.

7.6 5S 管理

5S Housekeeping

井然有序的生产车间是维持生产流程高品质的基本要求。由于生产物料成本占产品成本的85%以上，我们必须确保很好地控制物料的使用。 这其中的第一要务是保持整洁和次序。

Maintaining order on the shop floor is essential to maintaining high quality in the production process. With materials constituting up to 85% of the cost of the product, we need to make sure that our materials are controlled exceptionally well and the first way is by keeping everything neat and orderly.

生产制造的另外一个重要的事实是，我们在生产设备方面花费了巨大的 投资，我们必须保证生产设备处于最佳状态。生产设备的维护始于保持设备的清洁，不将物料或文件放置在设备上，正确地除尘和预防性的维护。

Another important aspect of manufacturing is the fact that manufacturing equipment is our largest capital investment and we need to keep it in top condition. Maintaining the equipment begins with keeping it clean, from not placing material and documents on the machine to proper vacuuming and preventive maintenance.

每一个捷普的成员都必须理解工厂外观的重要性和它对品质和生产的影响。

Every member of the Jabil team needs to understand the importance of plant appearance and its impact on quality and production.

客户往往因第一印象改变决定，留给客户第一印象的机会只有一次，这 将决定我们是赢得还是失去该项业务。

A customer can be swayed by a first impression and there is only one opportunity to make a first impression, which can be the difference in winning or losing business.

作为一项基本要求，捷普广州已经在从生产车间到办公区域建立起5S系统。5S 是：

5S System has been established as the baseline from the shop floor to office area in Jabil. The 5S are:

1. 分开处理（常组织）

Sort

2. 定量定位（常整顿）

Set-In-Order

3. 清洁检查（常清洁）

Shine

4. 立法守规（常规范）

Standardize

5. 保持维护（常自律）

Sustain

7.7 门禁和安全指引

Access control and Security Guideline

员工在进入公司厂区时，应出示厂证。

Employees are required to show their employee badge before entering the plant area.

根据职位和功能部门，每一个员工在生产及办公场所有各自的授权进出区域。员工进入未经授权的区域前必须得到相关部门的部门经理批准。

In manufacturing shop floor and office area, each employee will have an access authorization based on his/her position and function. Employee who needs to enter manufacturing/office area beyond the normal authorization must get approval from related Functional Manager.

在公司各出入口及厂区范围内的当值保安员，负责执行公司各项安全保卫规章制度和公司的安全保卫工作。所有保安已经接受专门的培训并被授权保护公司的财产和员工的人身安全。你会发现他们谦逊和有帮助。

保安员的工作需要你的理解和配合。

The uniformed security personnel stationed at points of entry or within a plant are charged with the responsibility of enforcing all regulations pertaining to plant protection. They have been trained for their jobs and have authority to act to protect company property as well as employee's safety. You will find them courteous and helpful. Their job will be made easier by your understanding and cooperation.

7.8 访客程序

Visitor Procedure

所有公司正式访客，包括客户，供应商或当地政府官员应受到最友好和及时的接待。同时，出于安全原因，员工应遵守以下访客指引：

All official visitors i.e. customers, suppliers or local authorities, etc. shall be treated in the most hospitable and expeditious fashion. However, for security reasons, employee shall follow below guide for visitors:

访客应在公司门卫处登记。访客应申报随身物品中所有与公司产品或资产相类似的物品如电脑，投影机，电路板等。

Visitors must register at the gate of the plant. Visitors shall declare any belongings with similar nature of company product or property such as computer, projector, PCB etc.

访客应在公司前台的区域等候直至被访人出面迎候。在访问期间，访客应由相应人员陪同。

Visitors will remain in the waiting area of reception until they have been greeted and chaperoned by the appropriate employee while in the facility.

未经部门经理许可，访客不允许进入生产车间。

No visitor shall be allowed into a production area without approval of the Functional Manager.

7.9 公司物品

Company Property

员工必须确保公司的物品在没有得到适当的授权或安检的情况下不能被携带外出。无论何种原因，携带任何公司物品外出必须开具相应的“公司物品出闸纸”。

Employees are to ensure that no company property is taken out without proper authorization or inspected by security. Any company property that needs to be taken out for whatever reason shall be accompanied by an “Out-going Goods Pass”.

7.10 信息安全和风险管理政策

Information Security & Risk Management Policy

信息管理和计算机处理系统在捷普商业中被广泛的使用。为确保捷普商业的成功，对这些系统的有效性、保密性、完整性的保护是十分必需的和必要的。

员工均有义务有责任去确保信息和所有的资源（如电脑和信息系统）是被保护的，不会被未经授权的人使用。

严禁员工未经允许发送、传播、散布或用其他途径或方式泄露属于捷普公司或捷普公司客户所有的信息、数据、商业机密或者其他的机密信 息。

员工不能期望存贮在系统里的文件或电子表单等的隐私权受到保护。被授权的员工可以查阅相关内容是否遵守法律法规，被授权的员工也可对网络进行监控。

公司提供的信息管理系统和设备是公司的财产，它意味着仅仅只能作为公司商业的目的，员工不能滥用这些系统和设备。

当员工需要下载、保存和使用软件、文件、图表、消息和其他的信息时有责任遵守版权法和相应的法律法规。

员工需要确保授权的帐号不泄露和密码的复杂化，以确保不被未经授权的人使用。

Information management and computer system have been widely used in Jabil business. To ensure the success of Jabil business, protection of the effectiveness, confidentiality and integrity of these systems are pretty essential and necessary.

Employees are responsibility to ensure that information and all resources (such as computer and information system) are protected and would not be used by an unauthorized person.

Employees must not allowed to send, transmit, distribute, or use other means or methods to disclose all of the information, data, trade secrets or other confidential information which belongs to the Company or clients without permission.

Employee may not expect that documents stored in the system or in electronic form are private or protected. Authorized employee can access to check whether or not the relevant content is in compliance with laws and regulations, and also can monitor the network.

Information management systems and equipment provided by company are subjected to company property which means they can only be used for the business purpose. Employees should not abuse these systems and equipment When employees need to download, save and use of software, documents, forms, message and other information, they have responsibility to comply with the copyright law and relative regulations.

Employees need to ensure that their authorized account is not disclosed and their passwords are complex to avoid unauthorized person using them.
