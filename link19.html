QUALITY

8.1 质量方针 Quality Policy

捷普的目标是提供满足或超出客户期望的质量解决方案.目标的达成有赖于管理层的领导,满足质量要求,投资于先进的制程及接受挑战性的商业目标。

捷普授权全体员工事事尽善尽美,以利于全球的客户,股东和员工。

核心程序的标准化和精益制造原则使得捷普可以提前识别,了解并快速改善质量和运作问题. 通过对人员,过程和技术的投入,捷普持续保持稳定的质量, 由此确保向客户按时交付价格有竞争力的合格产品。

The goal of Jabil is to provide quality solutions that meet or exceed our customer's expectations. This is achieved through management leadership, compliance to quality requirements, investment in cutting-edge processes, and accepting challenging business objectives.

Jabil team members are empowered to drive continual improvement in everything they do. This engagement benefits customers, shareholders and employees worldwide.

Standardization of core processes and lean principles allows Jabil to proactively identify, understand, and rapidly resolve quality and operational issues. Jabil continues to “build in” quality through investment in our people, processes and technology. This ensures competitive cost, quality products, and on-time delivery performance to our customers.

8.2 个人静电防护规则

Basic Personal ESD Rules

生产车间、来料检查区、货仓和实验室为静电防护区域，必须采取基本ESD 防护措施。 静电防护区域可分为非静电操作区域和静电操作区域，静电操作区域又可分为0 级(静电控制less than 50V)、I 级(静电控制less than100V)、II 级(静电控制less than 2000V)三个级别，不同级别的静电操作区域须相应采取额外ESD 防护措施。

Basic ESD protection practices must observed in ESD protection area including workshop, IQC, Store & Lab. EPA divided into non-EHA and EHA(ESDS handling area), EHA have 3 classification: 0(target less than 50V), I(target less than100V), II(target less than2000V), different EHA area has corresponding additional ESD protection requirement .

元器件与b配件除非已确认为报废或为屏蔽包装，不可以放置于非静电操作区，严禁拿到静电防护区外。

Component & PCBA can not be placed in non-EHA area, and are also prohibited to be taken out of ESD protection area with the exception of approval scraps or shielding packages.

直接生产员工进入静电防护区域时必须穿著工衣直接生产员工进入静电防护区域时必须穿著工衣、帽子和防静电工鞋，以之作为基本ESD 防 护措施。

Direct production operator must wear full gears (Smock & Cap, ESD shoes) as basic ESD protection when enter EPA area.

非直接生产人员或访客进入静电防护区域时，如头发长过6 英寸(15cm),除穿著ESD 工衣、工鞋/脚跟带外，需另著ESD 帽子并将头发扎起塞进帽子里。

Non direct staff with hair length than 6'' (15cm), need to wear ESD cap ,tied up hair and tack inside the cap when they enable the EPA while wearing a smock and shoes /heel strip.

在进入静电防护区域时，只有经理(著白色工衣)、客人(著黄色工衣)、供应商(著深蓝色工衣)可以使用ESD 脚跟带，捷普（广州）的其他员 工应穿ESD 工鞋而不是脚跟带。

Only managers (wearing smock in white), client (in yellow), vendor (in dark blue) can wear ESD heel strips when enter EPA area, Jabil (Guangzhou) other employees should wear ESD shoes instead of ESD heel strip.

所有人员必须正确通过ESD 自动测试系统进入生产车间，不允许如跳入闸系统、没有测试跟随前面的人员进入、侧身从旁边通道处进入，钻过十字转门等形式进入车间。

Personnel must pass ESD auto check systems correctly before entering workshop, please do not jump over ESD auto gate, following the person in front without checking ESD, bypass ESD gate, go under the turnstile etc.

防静电服装的清洗管理由人力资源部负责，ESD 工衣由专业洗衣公司每月至少清洗一次，严禁私自用普通的清洗方法清洗。ESD 工鞋由个人自行清洗，建议每月至少清洗一次。

ESD gears cleanliness is managed by the HR dept. ESD smock should be cleaned once per month by a professional laundry company; it is forbidden to be cleaned by the employee through general method. ESD shoes may be cleaned by owner; it is recommended they are cleaned at least once per month.

防静电工衣和工鞋不能穿出换鞋区，静电防护区，办公区和饭厅，更不允许穿到吸烟房和厂房外(紧急情况除外)。

No ESD smock and shoes are allowed outside the shoes cabinet area, EPA area， office area and Canteen area, they are forbidden to the Smock Room and out of the building (exceptional in emergencies).

当需要进入静电防护区域时,访客陪同接待人员应向访客传达此ESD 防护守则。

If need to enter EPA area, visitor's receptionist should convey this personal ESD rules to respective visitor.

所有人员包括访客必须遵循此个人静电防护守则。

All personnel, including visitor, need to follow the personal ESD rules.
