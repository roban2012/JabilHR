第九节：员工沟通

COMMUNICATION

良好的沟通，是捷普活力的源泉。我们鼓励开放与坦诚的双向沟通，并竭尽全力使员工获得与他们工作、生活相关的各个方面的即时信息；同时鼓励员工提出反馈意见。

Communication is the lifeblood of Jabil. We firmly believe in open and honest 2-way communication and strive to ensure employees have real-time information on issues that affect them in every aspect of their work life and that they have the opportunity to provide feedback on these issues.

9.1 广开言路政策

Open Door Policy

广开言路政策的目的在于在捷普的每一家工厂都能够确保有意义的双向沟通。它允许员工向管理层提出自己关注的问题及改善的建议。双向沟通开放政策下应遵循以下的申述渠道：

The purpose of the Open Door Policy is to ensure meaningful 2-way communication exists in each of Jabil plant. It allows employees to suggest improvements, highlight issues and raise concerns to the management teams. The escalation path within the Open Door Policy is as follows:

你的直接主管

Your immediate Supervisor

你的部门经理和/或人力资源部经理

Your Functional Manager and / or HR Manager

你的营运经理

Your Operations Manager

我们鼓励员工使用上述的申述渠道。这样能够使你的主管和部门经理在问题申述至营运经理前解决它。然而如果你相信你需要绕过其中的某些步骤，我们也鼓励你这样做并倾听你解释原因。

We encourage you to use the escalation path in the above order as it allows supervisors and managers to fix problems prior to them being escalated to the Operations Manager.

However, if you believe you need to skip a step we encourage you to do so and then explain your reasoning.

9.2 季度业绩回顾

Quarterly Business Review

季度公司业绩回顾大会的目的在于向所有员工沟通与工厂相关的一些信息。大会中的主要议题包括：本厂最新情况，项目，客户信息，公司总部的最新情况和公司的分红信息。

The purpose of the Quarterly Business Review is to communicate relevant plant information to all employees. The typical information communicated during this meeting is; local plant updates, program and customer information, corporate updates and profit share information.

季度公司业绩回顾大会通常会在捷普一个财政季度结束之后安排。大会将选定一天内进行，以确保所有员工收到及时和一致的信息。我们鼓励所有部门和班次员工参加季度公司业绩回顾大会。

Quarterly Business Reviews are typically held as soon as is reasonably practical following Jabil's Quarter end and are normally delivered to all employees over a one-day period to ensure that employees receive a consistent and timely message. All employees across all shifts and functions are encouraged to attend and participate in these meetings.

9.3 茶话会

Tea Talk

茶话会是捷普沟通战略的其中一项重要内容。目的是通过一种非正式和轻松的方式从员工中收集与工作相关的反馈。从茶话会中获得的反馈对评估员工士气，识别工厂运作的成功与待改进之处尤为重要。

Round Tables (Tea talk) are one of the most important mechanisms of Jabil's communication strategy. The purpose of Round Table meetings is to gain feedback from employees on work-related topics in an informal, relaxed setting. The feedback received from these meeting is essential in assessing employee morale and in identifying successes and areas for improvement at a plant level.

参加茶话会的员工应包括直接生产工人和非直接生产工人，会议应由10 到12 名员工代表参加。会议通常会由运作经理或人力资源部经理/ 部门经理主持。员工可以在会议上提出任何议题，问题或者所关注的重要事项。

Each Round Table will typically include DL and IL employees, targeting representative groups of 10-12 employees. The meetings will normally be facilitated by Operation Manager or HR Manager/Functional Manager. Employees are encouraged to raise any topics, questions or concerns that are important to them.

9.4 通告栏

Notice Board

通告栏设置在厂区各处。通告栏用于提供班次调整，政策变动等一般信息。

These are situated throughout the plant. These are used to provide information on shift adjustment, changes to policy and general information.

9.5 捷普之窗

Jabil Magazine

捷普之窗是捷普内部出版的月刊， 报道公司最近的动态及娱乐消遣活动，也欢迎员工们积极的投稿。

Window of Jabil is Jabil's internally circulated magazine that contains updates of the company's activities as well as leisure activities for employees. Most articles are welcome to becontributed by fellow employees.

9.6 生产部“开拉”会议 Manufacturing “start of shift” meetings

“开拉”会议将向各条生产线的生产工人传达当天的生产目标，有关品质和客户的各项事项。

These meetings are designed to provide Manufacturing Operators on each bay with information on the targets for the day, quality issues and customer issues.

9.7 捷普内部网

JabilWeb

JabilWeb 是捷普公司的内部网，是与全球各地的捷普员工分享信息的沟通工具。内部网的内容包括营运发展，各功能部门职能介绍，捷普大学图书馆和公司信息包括公司地址，员工名录，投资者关系，旅行信息。

A communications tool for sharing globally relevant information with Jabil employees, JabilWeb is the Corporate Intranet. Content includes Operations Development, Functional Cookbooks and Jabil University Library, company information, which includes material such as Location Information, Employee Directory, Investor Relations, and Travel information.

Jabil.com and International Websites

作为一项向社会公众宣传捷普公司和捷普解决方案的沟通工具，jabil.com 是公司的国际互联网站。Jabil.com 的主要浏览者是投资 者，潜在客户和员工及传媒。捷普国际互联网站的网址是：

http://www.jabil.com。

A communications tool promoting the company and its solutions to the public, jabil.com is the Corporate Internet site. The core audience for jabil.com is Investors, Potential Customers and Employees, and Media. Jabil.com can be found at http://www.jabil.com.

9.9 捷普全球互联网广播

Internal Worldwide Webcast

全球互联网广播向全球的捷普工厂现场直播，广播向所有捷普员工传达公司新闻和公司目标。这一活动每年举办一次，全球的捷普员工可以通过这一活动向捷普管理层提交问题并进行沟通。互联网广播的内容和索引将在JabilWeb 上存档。

Broadcast live to every Jabil site, the Webcast conveys company news, goals and objectives to all employees of Jabil Circuit. The annual event gives employees an opportunity to communicate with Jabil's management, with executives fielding questions submitted from Jabil employees around the world. Webcasts are indexed and archived on JabilWeb.

9.10 年报摘要

Summary Annual Reports

公司年报摘要将由捷普沟通部门经每一个工厂的人力资源部经理提供给每一位员工。年报包含一封由行政长官写给全球捷普员工的信件，回顾公司过往一年的业绩，讨论公司的前景和基本财务信息。

Summary Annual Reports are provided to every employee by Corporate Communications. They are distributed through the HR Managers at each plant. The Annual Report includes an executive letter reviewing the past year's performance and discussing the company's outlook as well as basic financial information.

9.11 新闻公报

Press Releases

公司将定期通过商业媒体进行新闻发布。新闻发布的内容将通过电子邮件发送到全球各个角落。新闻发布的打印稿将发给那些无法使用电子邮件的员工。

Periodically, Jabil issues press releases through Business Wire. Once those releases are issued, they are sent worldwide via the Internet, and hard copies are provided to employees without e-mail access.
