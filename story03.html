欢迎加入捷普

Welcome to Jabil

祝贺您加入捷普！不论您是何种职位，您现在已成为捷普大家庭中的重要一员；捷普的成功依赖于您的工作和热情。

点击查看捷普的愿景。

本员工手册将让您全面地了解捷普公司。我们希望这本手册能够以友好和简明的方式帮助您熟悉捷普。

然而请理解，公司对本手册所陈述的各项条款拥有完全的解释权；并在必要和恰当的前提下，酌情对各项条款的解释、管理和适用性进行相应的处理。本员工手册已征询工会意见，经过民主程序制定，并通过签收送达或培训方式向员工告知。

如果本手册所陈述的内容与公司的正式文件（包括，但不限于劳动合同，录用通知，公司政策和程序等）的陈述不一致，以公司的正式文件为准。

以下为《员工手册》节选。

Congratulations on joining Jabil. Whatever your position, you are now an important member of the large team of people whose work and enthusiasm make Jabil a success.

This employee handbook will provide you with a general understanding on Jabil. We intend this booklet to be a guide to help familiarize you with Jabil as a company in a friendly, easy to refer format.

However, please understand the Company has the discretionary authority and responsibility to interpret the terms provisions and to exercise discretion where necessary or appropriate in the interpretation, administration and determination of eligibility for the terms or package. Through democratic procedures, this booklet has been consulted with Jabil Labor Union after discussion with Labor Union. Jabil employees will be informed the contents of this booklet by sign the acknowledgement of receipt of this booklet or through training.

If there are any differences between this booklet and the company official documents (include, but not limited to, employment contract, offer letter, Company policies and procedure etc.), the documents prevail.
